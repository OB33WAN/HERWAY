Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about example journeys, bespoke requests, scuba planning, and advisor support.

What is included in an example journey?

Each example journey includes destination routing, highlights, a sample itinerary, and starting price guidance. Final quotes depend on exact dates, certification requirements, and availability.

Can I adjust an example journey itinerary?

Yes. Example journey enquiries are designed to be flexible. We can modify hotel level, pacing, activities, route complexity, and scuba-diving requirements.

What should I include in a bespoke request?

Share destination ideas, travel dates, budget, number of travellers, and must-haves such as trip style, pace, and accommodation standards.

How long does a bespoke proposal take?

Most first proposals are returned within 3-5 business days depending on complexity and destination seasonality.

Are example journey prices fixed?

No. Displayed prices are starting prices or ranges to help comparison. Final pricing is confirmed with your exact dates, inclusions, and selections.

Can you plan scuba-diving holidays for mixed-ability groups?

Yes. I can shape dive and non-dive balance, resort transfers, certification needs, and surface activities so mixed-interest groups travel well together.

Do you charge a planning fee?

Some bespoke and concierge planning requests include a planning fee, which is always clarified before work starts.

Send a message and include your travel brief for a fast response.
