GDPR Data Rights

This page explains your rights under the UK GDPR and EU GDPR when you use Wanderlust services or submit enquiries through this website.

1. Your Rights

  • Right of access to your personal data.
  • Right to rectify inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure, where legally applicable.
  • Right to restrict or object to processing.
  • Right to data portability for data you provided directly.
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time for consent-based processing.

2. Legal Bases for Processing

We process personal data under one or more of the following legal bases: consent, contractual necessity, legitimate interests, and legal obligation.

3. How to Submit a Request

Email requests to privacy@wanderlusttraveladvisory.com with the subject line "GDPR Request" and include sufficient details to verify identity and locate records.

4. Response Time

Requests are acknowledged promptly and typically fulfilled within one month, subject to lawful extensions for complex requests.

5. Complaints

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority, including the UK Information Commissioner's Office where relevant.
