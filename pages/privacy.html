Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Wanderlust collects and processes personal data when you browse this website, submit package enquiries, or request bespoke trip planning support.

1. Data Controller

Wanderlust acts as data controller for information collected through this website and direct advisory communications.

2. Information We Collect

  • Identity and contact details such as name, email, and phone number.
  • Travel preferences including destinations, dates, traveler numbers, and budget ranges.
  • Correspondence data from forms and advisory communications.
  • Consent and cookie preference records.

3. How We Use Information

  • To respond to enquiries and deliver advisory services.
  • To design and refine package and bespoke travel proposals.
  • To meet legal and compliance obligations.
  • To improve content and usability where analytics consent is provided.

4. Legal Bases

Personal data is processed under contractual necessity, legitimate interests, consent (where required), and legal obligations.

5. Data Sharing

Data is not sold. Information may be shared with travel suppliers and trusted service providers where necessary to prepare or deliver requested itinerary support.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as needed for service delivery, operational records, and legal compliance. Retention periods may vary by data category.

7. Your Rights

You may exercise rights of access, correction, erasure, restriction, objection, and portability where applicable. See GDPR Rights for full details.

8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Cookie usage is explained in the Cookie Policy. You can update preferences at any time using the Manage Cookies option.

9. Security

Reasonable technical and organisational safeguards are used to protect data against unauthorised access, alteration, and loss.

10. Contact

Privacy requests can be sent to privacy@wanderlusttraveladvisory.com.
