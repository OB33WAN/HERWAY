Cookie Policy

This policy explains how Wanderlust uses cookies and similar technologies on this website.

1. What Cookies Are Used

  • Essential cookies: required for core site functionality and accessibility features.
  • Analytics cookies: optional cookies that help understand visitor behavior and improve content and journeys.
  • Marketing cookies: optional cookies used only where campaign attribution or remarketing is active.

2. Consent and Control

On your first visit, you can accept all cookies, reject optional cookies, or set custom preferences. You can reopen controls any time using the Manage Cookies button in the footer.

3. Cookie Duration

Cookie consent preferences are stored in local storage with a timestamp and can be updated at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Where third-party services are used for analytics or forms, those providers may set or read cookies according to their own privacy notices. Optional analytics and marketing scripts should remain blocked until the relevant consent category has been accepted.

5. Consent-Aware Script Loading

This website supports deferred third-party scripts using consent categories. Scripts marked for analytics or marketing are only activated after matching consent is granted.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@wanderlusttraveladvisory.com.
