About HERWAY

HERWAY is a women-by-women taxi service dedicated to safe, professional, and reliable transport. We prioritize female driver matching while maintaining a vetted network that may include male drivers when requested or operationally required.

Our mission is simple: female passengers should travel with confidence, and all bookings are reviewed with clear passenger-context disclosures where others are traveling (for example an elderly grandfather or two teenage boys).

Our Safety Standards & Assurance

We maintain comprehensive safety standards that exceed industry requirements. Here's how we ensure your peace of mind:

🔍 Driver Verification Process

Identity Verification

All drivers submit government-issued ID (passport or national ID) which is verified against official records.

Enhanced DBS Check

Every driver undergoes an Enhanced DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) check, which covers:

  • Conviction history
  • Caution history
  • Criminal records information
  • Police information

Driving Record Check

We verify clean driving records with no serious violations in the past 3 years.

Reference Checks

Professional references are obtained and verified from previous employers.

🚗 Vehicle Standards

Regular Inspection

All vehicles undergo regular safety inspections to ensure roadworthiness and passenger comfort.

Valid MOT

Vehicles must maintain current MOT certificates with no serious defects.

Insurance Requirements

Full Private Hire Vehicle (PHV) insurance required at all times. No exceptions.

Vehicle Standards

Vehicles are typically 2015 or newer, well-maintained, and clean.

📋 Licensing & Compliance

PVH License Requirement

All drivers must have a valid Private Hire Vehicle (PVH) license issued by their local council.

Regulatory Compliance

We comply with all UK regulations regarding taxi and private hire services.

Data Protection

We comply fully with GDPR and data protection regulations. Your personal information is secure.

👮 Passenger Safety Policies

Right to Refuse

Drivers have the absolute right to refuse or cancel any trip if they have safety concerns.

Clearly Communicated Policies

All passengers receive clear communication of our policies, including restrictions on undisclosed passengers.

Child Safety

We require proper car seats for children. Passengers must declare children at booking.

24/7 Support

Both drivers and passengers have access to 24/7 support for any issues or concerns.

How HERWAY Works

1

You Book

Contact us via WhatsApp, our contact form, or phone with your travel requirements including pickup location, destination, date, and time.

2

We Verify

If booking passengers with others (men, children), let us know at booking so we can prepare appropriately and match you with the right driver.

3

We Quote

We provide a transparent quote within 30 minutes, including any additional costs like airport parking. You'll see the full breakdown.

4

You Confirm

Confirm your booking and you'll receive driver details in advance so you know who will be collecting you.

5

We Deliver

Your driver arrives on time, professional and ready to provide safe, courteous transportation to your destination.

6

You Review

After your journey, you can rate your driver and share feedback to help us continually improve our service.
