Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a taxi with HERWAY?

You can book through WhatsApp or our booking form. HERWAY is women by women and female-first, so please include clear passenger context if anyone else is traveling (for example an elderly grandfather or two teenage boys).

What if I need immediate transportation?

Contact us via WhatsApp for the fastest response time. We often can arrange pickups within 30-60 minutes depending on driver availability in your area.

Do you operate outside of regular hours?

We offer flexible booking times. Contact us to check availability for your required date and time - we aim to accommodate requests whenever possible.

What about airport parking charges?

For airports like Heathrow with short-stay parking fees, these charges are included in your final quote. We'll provide a complete breakdown with no hidden costs.

Are there discounts for regular bookings?

We offer competitive rates for regular bookings like school runs or weekly appointments. Contact us to discuss special rates for your needs.

How do I know the driver is qualified?

All HERWAY drivers are DBS checked, licensed PVH drivers with valid insurance. We maintain strict quality and safety standards.

What if I need a car seat for my child?

Let us know at booking if you need a car seat. We can arrange this in advance to ensure your child's safety and comfort.

How do I become a HERWAY driver?

Visit our "Become a Driver" page to learn about requirements and apply. The process includes application, document verification, DBS check, and online interview.

Do you operate in my area?

We're expanding across the UK. Contact us to check if we operate in your location or to express interest in having us service your area.

Service Areas

We currently operate around West London, mainly from Hounslow to Harrow and toward Slough/nearby areas. Contact us to check coverage for your exact pickup and destination.

For expansion plans or to request service outside the Hounslow-Harrow-Slough coverage zone, please get in touch using any of our contact methods above.
