# Herway Legal & Regulatory Compliance Summary

## Overview
This document summarizes all UK legal and regulatory requirements covered by the Herway website, particularly in the new Legal & Compliance page and related documentation.

## ✅ Legal & Regulatory Frameworks Implemented

### 1. TAXI & TRANSPORT LICENSING
- **Private Hire Vehicle (PHV) License** ✓
  - All vehicles licensed with local authorities
  - Regular compliance monitoring
  
- **Private Hire Driver (PVH) License** ✓
  - All drivers hold valid PVH licenses
  - Issued by local council after passing required checks
  
- **Operator License** ✓
  - Company holds necessary operator license
  - Compliance with local authority requirements

- **Vehicle Compliance Standards** ✓
  - Safety & suitability standards met
  - Accessibility requirements

- **MOT & Roadworthiness** ✓
  - Current MOT certificates required
  - Regular safety inspections conducted
  - No serious defects

- **Transparent Pricing** ✓
  - Accurate quotes provided before service
  - Clear pricing structure

### 2. INSURANCE & FINANCIAL SECURITY

### 3. SAFEGUARDING & BACKGROUND CHECKS
- **Enhanced DBS Checks** ✓
  - All drivers undergo Enhanced DBS
  - Checks conviction, caution, police information, bar list entries
  - Required for roles with public contact

- **Barred Lists Verification** ✓
  - Verification not on children's barred list
  - Verification not on adults' barred list

- **Driving License Verification** ✓
  - DVLA check on all drivers
  - Verification of valid UK license
  - No serious violations in 3 years

- **Reference Checks** ✓
  - Previous employer references obtained
  - References verified from multiple sources

- **Identity Verification** ✓
  - Government-issued ID verification
  - Passport or national ID required

- **Safeguarding Policy** ✓
  - Policy to protect vulnerable passengers
  - Risk assessments conducted

- **Lone Working Policy** ✓
  - Enhanced safety protocols for drivers
  - Night shift lone working procedures

### 4. DATA PROTECTION & PRIVACY

#### UK GDPR Compliance ✓
- Data Protection Act 2018 compliance
- Privacy impact assessments conducted
- Data protection by design principles

#### Personal Data Handling
- **Consent Management** ✓
  - Explicit consent obtained for marketing
  - Consent records maintained
  
- **Data Security** ✓
  - SSL/TLS encryption for transmission
  - Hashed & salted password storage
  - Access controls implemented
  - Data breach response plan

- **Data Subject Rights** ✓
  - Right of access (Subject Access Requests)
  - Right to rectification
  - Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  - Right to restrict processing
  - Right to data portability
  - Right to object
  - Right to withdraw consent
  
- **Breach Notification** ✓
  - Incident response plan (72-hour notification)
  - ICO notification procedures
  - Individual notification procedures

#### Third-Party Sharing
- Limited sharing with necessary parties
- Data Processing Agreements in place
- No international data transfers without safeguards

## 🛠️ BEFORE DEPLOYMENT

### Critical Actions:
1. **Update Contact Information**
   - Replace compliance email address
   - Add phone contact
   - Add physical address

2. **Set Up Compliance Infrastructure**
   - DBS application process
   - Insurance policies in place
   - PVH license application support

3. **Legal Review**
   - Have lawyer review all documents
   - Ensure local authority compliance
   - Verify GDPR implementation

4. **Implementation Checklist**
   - [ ] All drivers have DBS clearance
   - [ ] Vehicles have PHV insurance
   - [ ] Drivers have PVH licenses
   - [ ] Website HTTPS enabled (GDPR requirement)
   - [ ] Privacy policy reviewed
   - [ ] Terms of Service approved
   - [ ] Complaints procedure documented
   - [ ] Data protection officer appointed (if needed)
